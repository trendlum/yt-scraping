Design a high-end research lab dashboard for YouTube intelligence and opportunity scouting.

The product must help users discover:
- niches of interest
- channels of interest
- videos of interest
- topic clusters worth replicating

This dashboard is for analytical decision-making, not casual reporting.

VISUAL DIRECTION
- Hybrid style: premium dark base with subtle light surfaces inside cards
- Looks like a research cockpit
- Strong contrast and clear hierarchy
- Neutral palette with one positive accent and one risk accent
- Muted teal or green for positive signal
- Amber for caution
- Red only for critical warning
- Avoid generic SaaS styling
- Avoid purple-first branding
- Dense, serious, deliberate

PLACEHOLDER STYLE GUIDELINES
- Use realistic placeholder data so the layout feels like a real product
- Mix short labels with longer item names
- Show numbers, deltas, and confidence badges in the placeholders
- Include meaningful sample values, not lorem ipsum
- Use placeholders that make ranking, filtering, and scanning easy to test

DATA RULES
- Use tables for ranked entities
- Use compact sparklines for trends
- Use score cards for normalized metrics
- Use badges for categorical states
- Always show confidence where sample size is weak
- Do not overcomplicate charts

INTERACTIONS
- clicking a niche filters channels and topics
- clicking a channel filters videos
- clicking a topic filters videos
- clicking a video opens detail drawer
- filters persist across screens
- sorting available on all ranking tables

RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR
- desktop-first
- tablet support
- mobile filter drawer
- ranking tables become cards
- detail drawers become sheets

CONSTRAINTS
- do not create a generic admin dashboard
- do not overuse decorative charts
- do not bury confidence indicators
- do not make raw counts the primary story
- do not mix niches, channels, topics, and videos into one list

INFORMATION PRIORITY
1. replicability
2. sustained traction
3. growth direction
4. confidence / sample quality
5. opportunity type
6. raw counts

This should feel like a premium analytical workspace for scouting opportunity, with strong visual logic and clear decision support.
